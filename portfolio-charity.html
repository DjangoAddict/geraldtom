Introduction / Greenburgh

Through an incredibly difficult time for our country, this was a great experience leading a team that quickly grew from a one man effort into a 10+ person team which included fundraising, sourcing and distribution efforts. Although incredibly difficult at the time, through our combined resources, we were able to get 501-c charity status through a sponsorship and source incredibly difficult to obtain masks. In the end, we were able donate to 2 hospitals, individual nurses, doctors and the Emergency Medical Services unit of the Greenburgh Police Department.
